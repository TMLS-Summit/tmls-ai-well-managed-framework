# Project Milestones and Contribution Timeline

This document outlines the key milestones and the phased contribution approach for the TMLS AI Well-Managed Framework. 

## Key Project Milestones

- **Now – End of April**: Candidate shortlist finalized, outreach begins.
- **Mid-May**: 2–3 committed contributors confirmed per pilot pillar (Compliance & Governance, Security).
- **Mid-May**: PR contribution guidelines live on GitHub.
- **End of May**: First contributor PRs submitted, framework content seeded.
- **June 17/18**: Soft showcase at TMLS Summit.
- **End of August**: All 7 pillars have at least one active contributor.
- **October**: Framework reaches sufficient maturity for external validation.
- **Nov 17–18**: Formal public announcement at MLOps World Austin.

## Contribution Timeline (Phased Rollout)

### Phase 1 (April–May): Pilot
Pilot with 2 pillars (Compliance & Governance, Security). 
- Outreach to 3–5 senior candidates each. 
- **Goal**: 2–3 committed contributors per pillar who submit an initial PR.

### Phase 2 (June–August): Expansion
Expand to the remaining 5 pillars.
- Each pillar targets a single Pillar Lead plus a small squad (max 5–7 people).

### Phase 3 (Sept–Oct): Review & Validation
- Framework consolidation.
- Peer review across all pillars.
- External validation with enterprise partners.

### Phase 4 (November): Launch
- Public announcement at MLOps World Austin.
